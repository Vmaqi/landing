FAQ

Odyssey of Gods- Anubis Rising is a unique collection of 777 limited edition NFTs (Non-Fungible Tokens) built on the Ethereum blockchain. Each NFT represents a digital generative PFP by @Inkartluis. At its core, it features a blend of rarities, access and collectibility while offering an engaging experience to holders.

These NFTs grant holders exclusive access to a web3 comic book and ownership of the intellectual property (IP) associated with the NFT.

What is the contract you will be using?

We will be utilizing the ERC-721A contract standard, which is an extension of the widely-adopted ERC-721 standard. This enables the mint to be friendly in terms of GAS spent, while maintaining compatibility with popular Ethereum-based NFT marketplaces and wallets.

How can I participate in the sale?

You have two options. One is to configure a web3 compatible wallet (We recommend Metamask) and fund it with enough ETH to participate in the mint plus Gas. The other option is to use the implemented crossmint solution that allows you to mint NFTs using your bank card and store them in a wallet facilitated by Crossmint.

Your NFT grants you the freedom to explore, but the heart of our cosmic odyssey and the essence of Anubis belong to us. Together, let's navigate this boundless universe while respecting the creative territories we inhabit!
